# How to Create Your First Agent

This tutorial will guide you through the process of creating your first agent in Agentic Space.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you:
- Have an account in Agentic Space
- Are authenticated in the system

## Step 1: Access the Creation Area

1. Log in to your account
2. In the main menu, click on "Create Agent"
3. You will be redirected to the agent creation page

## Step 2: Configure Basic Information

Fill in the following fields:

- **Name**: A descriptive name for your agent
- **Description**: A brief explanation of what your agent does
- **Instructions**: The guidelines your agent will follow

![Configuration example](imagens/configuracao.png)

## Step 3: Define Capabilities

Select the capabilities your agent will have:
- Text processing
- Data analysis
- Content generation

## Step 4: Test and Publish

After configuring your agent:
1. Click on "Test" to verify the functionality
2. If everything is correct, click on "Publish"
3. Your agent will be available for use

## Additional Tips

- Keep instructions clear and objective
- Test different capabilities before publishing
- Regularly review your agent's settings

## Conclusion

Congratulations! You have created your first agent in Agentic Space. Continue exploring the available features.
